ABOUT US

HSquare is an Egyptian Company LLC. Established in 2019 providing a wide range of services such as Real-Estate Services, Marketing, Franchise Services, HR services, IT Solutions, Financial Consultancy, Economical Feasibility Studies, Investment Opportunities Assessment and relevant specialized and customized training programs.

VISION

Our target is to be one of the best management & consultancy companies that provide their clients with up-to-date, practical and applicable on-ground investment solutions; and to be the second-to-none choice for the investors whom are looking for integrity, quality and authenticity in their field of interest—"The Honest and Mighty".

MISSION

HSquare strives to:

-Have a valuable influence on the biggest number of individuals, investors, and companies which we serve in the realm of investment consultancy and marketing by maintaining, developing and empowering them. -Support our clients to master their businesses and look for potential opportunities and drive them to develop and maintain their business activities with fruitful results . -Empower our clients to achieve the highest possible revenue growth within their market sector business ethics

CORE VALUES

-Commit to provide the highest level of qualitative and ethical services to our clients. -Dedicate to meet our client's needs and going ahead of their expectations. -Strive to build and maintain strong professional and personal relationships with our clients and to gain their trust and satisfaction. -Rendered services through the best professional expertise implementing and delivering modern scientific methodology -Develop our performance persistently and look for every new market solution as convenient and applicable to our client sectors needs -Provide a work environment that attracts professional expertise, empowers them, and enriches the market with their on-ground experience
